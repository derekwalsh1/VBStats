Transform how you analyze volleyball performance with Ace Your Stats, the comprehensive statistics tracking app designed specifically for coaches, players, and team managers.

Record every rally outcome with intuitive, courtside-friendly controls. Track kills, errors, and defensive plays while the app automatically calculates scores and updates live statistics. No more paper scoresheets or manual calculations—focus on the game while Ace Your Stats handles the numbers.

**LIVE MOMENTUM VISUALIZATION**
Watch the match unfold through dynamic momentum charts that display score differential in real-time. Instantly identify scoring runs, momentum shifts, and critical turning points. Visualize when your team is dominating and when you need to make strategic adjustments.

**ROTATION ANALYSIS**
Discover which rotations are your strongest and which need work. Ace Your Stats automatically tracks your team's rotation throughout each set, calculating:
• Point scoring percentage per rotation
• Sideout percentage per rotation  
• Performance trends across different positions
• Serve vs. receive effectiveness by rotation

**TIMEOUT MANAGEMENT**
Never lose track of timeouts again. Visual indicators show exactly how many timeouts remain for both teams. In the 5th set, the app automatically adjusts to single timeout rules. Make strategic decisions with confidence.

## App Review Notes (for Apple reviewers)

**Demo Instructions:**

To fully test the app, follow these steps:

1. **Create a Team:**
   - Tap "Add Team" on the home screen
   - Enter any team name (e.g., "Demo Team")
   - Tap "Create"

2. **Create a Match:**
   - Tap the team tile
   - Tap "Add Match"
   - Enter opponent name (e.g., "Opponent Team")
   - Select today's date
   - Optionally enter an event name
   - Choose "Serve" or "Receive"
   - Tap "Create Match"

3. **Record a Set:**
   - Tap "Add Set"
   - Use the three outcome buttons to record rallies:
     * "Kill" - Your team wins the rally
     * "Error" - Your team makes an error
     * "Defense" - Opponent error or your team defends to win
   - Watch the score update automatically
   - Observe the momentum chart at the bottom
   - Try the undo button to reverse a rally
   - Try tapping T1 or T2 to record timeouts
   - Continue until one team reaches 25 points with a 2-point lead

4. **Review Statistics:**
   - After the set ends, you'll see detailed stats
   - View rotation breakdowns
   - See point scoring and sideout percentages

**Key Testing Notes:**
- App works completely offline (airplane mode is fine)
- No login or account required
- No external links or web browsing
- All data stored locally on device
- Long-press any tile to delete it
- Export/import accessible via menu (if implemented in UI)

**Privacy:**
- No data collection whatsoever
- No analytics or tracking
- No third-party services
- Safe for all ages including children

Thank you for reviewing Ace Your Stats!
