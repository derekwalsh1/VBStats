# Privacy Policy for Ace Your Stats

**Last Updated: December 28, 2025**

## Overview

Ace Your Stats ("the App") is committed to protecting your privacy. This privacy policy explains how we handle your information.

## Data Collection

VBStats does **not** collect, transmit, or share any personal information or usage data. All data is stored locally on your device only.

### What We Don't Collect

- Personal information (name, email, phone number, etc.)
- Location data
- Usage analytics or statistics
- Device identifiers
- Crash reports or diagnostics
- Any data transmitted to external servers

## Data Storage

All volleyball statistics, team information, match data, and settings are:

- Stored locally on your device using SQLite database
- Never transmitted over the internet
- Never shared with third parties
- Never backed up to our servers (we don't have any)
- Only accessible to you on your device

## Data Export

The App allows you to export your data as JSON files:

- You choose when and where to export
- Exported files are saved to your device's file system
- You maintain complete control over exported data
- No copies are sent to us or any third party

## Data Import

You can import previously exported data:

- Only you can import data to your device
- Imports are processed entirely on your device
- No internet connection required

## Third-Party Services

VBStats does not integrate with any third-party services, analytics platforms, or advertising networks.

## Children's Privacy

The App does not collect any information from anyone, including children under 13.

## Changes to Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date.

## Data Deletion

Since all data is stored locally on your device:

- You can delete all data by uninstalling the app
- You can delete individual teams, matches, or sets within the app
- No residual data remains on any external servers

You have complete ownership and control of all data generated within the App.
