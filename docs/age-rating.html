Content Description

Ace Your Stats is a volleyball statistics tracking application designed for coaches, players, and team managers. The app contains:

  • Sports statistics only - Tracks rally outcomes, scores, and performance metrics
  • Educational value - Helps users learn about volleyball strategy and performance analysis
  • No user-generated content - All data is entered by the device owner for their own use
  • No social features - No chat, messaging, or ability to interact with other users
  • Completely offline - No internet connectivity or online features
